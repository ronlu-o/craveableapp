Craveable
 privacy policy

Your Privacy Matters

Craveable is designed with privacy at its core. Your photos, filters, and camera data never leave your device.

Data We Don't Collect

Craveable does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. We do not:

  • Upload your photos to any server
  • Track your usage or behavior
  • Collect analytics or telemetry
  • Access your photo library without permission
  • Share data with third parties
  • Use advertising networks or trackers

Permissions We Request

Camera Access

Required to capture and preview photos with real-time filters. All processing happens locally on your device using AVFoundation and Core Image frameworks.

Motion & Orientation Data

Used to detect device orientation via accelerometer (CMMotionManager) to ensure photos are correctly oriented. This data is processed in real-time and never stored or transmitted.

Photo Library Access

Required only when you choose to save filtered photos to your device. You have full control over which photos are saved.

How Your Data is Processed

All photo processing, filter application, and orientation handling occurs entirely on your device:

  • Filters: Applied using GPU-accelerated processing via Core Image with cached LUT (Look-Up Table) files stored locally in the app.
  • Photos: Captured and processed using Apple's AVFoundation framework. Original and filtered versions remain on your device unless you choose to delete them.
  • Orientation: Device physical orientation is detected using accelerometer data to ensure proper photo rotation. This data is never logged or stored.
  • Camera Settings: Flash mode, zoom level, and filter preferences are stored locally using UserDefaults and never synced or backed up.

Third-Party Services

Craveable does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app operates completely independently.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, security is managed by iOS and your device's built-in protections. We recommend:

  • Keeping your iOS up to date
  • Using device passcode or biometric authentication
  • Managing photo library permissions in iOS Settings

Contact & Support

Children's Privacy

Craveable does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is designed for general audiences and processes all data locally on-device.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will notify users through the app or on this website. Continued use of Craveable after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
